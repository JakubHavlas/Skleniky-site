FAQ

Questions, answered.

When will it launch?

We're rolling out the first generation through the pilot program now. Pilot restaurants get the cabinet first, before general availability.

How much maintenance is required?

Almost none. Lighting and watering are automated. You spend a few minutes a week refilling water and harvesting.

Which herbs can be grown?

Basil, parsley, mint, coriander and other popular kitchen herbs — each with its own optimized growing recipe.

Does it require plumbing?

No plumbing. The cabinet has its own reservoir — you simply top it up. It only needs a standard power socket.

How much electricity does it use?

It's designed to be efficient, running on energy-saving LEDs. Exact figures are part of what we'll validate together during the pilot.

Can I become a pilot partner?

Yes — that's exactly who this is for. Apply above and we'll be in touch if it's a fit.
